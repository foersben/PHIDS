# Curated Examples

PHIDS includes a suite of validated, curated examples demonstrating critical engine phenomena. These examples act simultaneously as behavioral proofs, testing targets, and foundational templates for custom scenario authoring.

## Dry Shrubland Cycles (`examples/dry_shrubland_cycles.json`)
Demonstrates cyclic boom-and-bust predator/prey dynamics within a resource-scarce biotope.

## Meadow Defense (`examples/meadow_defense.json`)
Highlights local-tissue toxin deployment. Demonstrates severe localized attrition when swarms engage chemically-defended flora.

## Mixed Forest Understory (`examples/mixed_forest_understory.json`)
Exhibits multi-species competition and varied spatial dispersal patterns dictated by specialized biological properties and asymmetric trophic chains.

## Root Network Alarm Chain (`examples/root_network_alarm_chain.json`)
Focuses on the `mycorrhizal_relay`. Displays how non-attacked flora adjacent to an active predation site synthesize defensive responses via connected root networks bypassing typical airborne diffusion delays.

## Wind Tunnel Orchard (`examples/wind_tunnel_orchard.json`)
Leverages dynamic meteorological configurations to demonstrate anisotropic signal diffusion and directional, anisotropic seed dispersion via continuous wind vectors.
