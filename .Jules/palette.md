## 2024-06-14 - Placement List Spatial ARIA Context
**Learning:** When dealing with lists of spatial entities (like plants and swarms placed on a grid) where multiple generic "delete" or "remove" icon buttons are present, generic ARIA labels (e.g., "Remove plant") are insufficient for screen reader users as they lack context to distinguish between identical entities.
**Action:** Always include specific identifying context within the `aria-label` and `title` attributes. For coordinate-based UI lists, interpolating the entity's coordinates directly into the label (e.g., `aria-label="Remove plant at ({{ p.x }}, {{ p.y }})"`) provides crucial spatial orientation and disambiguates the buttons for assistive technologies. Combine this with explicit `focus-visible` styling (`focus:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-red-500 rounded`) to ensure full keyboard navigation support.
