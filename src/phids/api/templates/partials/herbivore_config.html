Herbivore Species Configuration Guide

Columns Explained:

  • 🆔 ID: Unique species index automatically assigned by the engine.
  • 🏷️ Name: Custom species name.
  • ❤️ Energy Min: The threshold below which a swarm becomes energy-stressed and risks starvation/extinction.
  • 🏃 Speed (Velocity): Movement distance or step size per simulation tick across the grid cells.
  • 🍽️ Eating Rate (Consumption): The rate of biomass/energy consumed per tick from compatible plants in the same cell.
  • ➗ Repr. Divisor: The energy divisor determines how offspring are split and initialized during reproduction/mitosis phases.
  • 🛡️ Morph. Adapt. (Morphological Adaptation): Resistance to physical plant defenses (0.0 to 1.0).
  • 🧪 Chem. Neut. (Chemical Neutralization): Ability to neutralize ingested toxins (0.0 to 1.0).
  • 🍂 Digest. Eff. (Digestive Efficiency): Ability to extract calories from tough matter.
  • 🛠️ Actions: Add new herbivore species (up to 16) or delete existing ones.

How to Apply Changes?

Modifying any value in a table field immediately triggers a background save to the server-side draft.

⚠️ Crucial: These edits will not affect the active running simulation loop until you click the 🔄 Load Draft button in the sticky top header, or restart/reset the simulation loop.
