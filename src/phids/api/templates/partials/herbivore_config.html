Herbivore Population & Adaptation Guide

Ecological Parameters Explained:

  • 🆔 ID & 🏷️ Name: Species identifier and classification for tracking in telemetry buffers.
  • ❤️ Energy Min (Starvation Limit): The absolute minimum energy threshold required per capita before the swarm enters a starvation state, incurring mortality (min 0.1).
  • 🏃 Speed (Velocity): Movement period in ticks between relocations (min 1). A value of 1 = moves every tick (fastest). A value of 5 = rests 4 ticks and moves on the 5th.
  • 🍽️ Eating Rate (Consumption): Biomass extracted per tick from host flora (min 0.1). This directly influences the Lotka-Volterra interaction strength and energy transfer between trophic levels.
  • ➗ Split Divisor: The energy multiplier governing swarm mitosis (min 0.1). When a swarm's gathered energy exceeds population * energy_min * divisor, the swarm reproduces (population increases), allowing explosive exponential growth during periods of abundant food.
  • 📈 Split Pop. (Log): Explicit threshold for swarm mitosis (0 disables). Uses logarithmic slider mapping. If set > 0, swarm splits strictly based on headcount.

Evolutionary Resistances (0.0 to 1.0):

  • 🛡️ Morph. Adapt.: Resistance to constitutive morphological defenses (e.g., thorns, trichomes). A value of 1.0 implies complete immunity, allowing unhindered feeding despite physical barriers.
  • 🧪 Chem. Neut.: Metabolic capability to bind, sequester, or excrete induced toxic secondary metabolites (e.g., alkaloids, glucosinolates). Higher values mitigate the lethal effects of airborne or localized toxins.
  • 🍂 Digest. Eff.: Efficiency of breaking down complex polymers (lignin, cellulose). Influences baseline nutritional yield during grazing.
