Field interpretation for substance definitions

ID indexes the simulation layer. Name is the operator-facing label. Type determines whether the entry is an airborne signal or a toxin mode. Signals diffuse in the biotope, while toxin effects are applied locally at defended plant cells.

Synth. is the synthesis latency in ticks before activation. Aftereff. is the post-trigger persistence window in ticks once the activating condition is no longer true.

Lethality β applies only to Lethal Toxin and controls per-tick casualty pressure as a function of local toxin intensity. Repel Walk k applies only to Repellent Toxin and sets the forced random-walk duration used to displace swarms from the defended location.

E/tick is the metabolic maintenance cost paid by the plant while the substance remains active. SAR enables irreversible activation (systemic acquired resistance mode), so the defense persists once initiated. Actions remove a definition and trigger dependent reference compaction.

Multi-factor activation logic (enemy thresholds, precursor substances, and composite condition trees) is configured in the ⚡ Trigger Rules editor.
