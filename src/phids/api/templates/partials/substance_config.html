Substance Configuration Guide

Substance Behaviors Explained:

  • 🧪 Type: Signals diffuse through cells to alert neighbors. Lethal Toxins kill herbivores. Repellent Toxins force herbivores to move away. Standard Toxins act as basic chemical deterrents.
  • ⏱️ Synth. Duration: Number of ticks the plant continues synthesizing the substance once triggered.
  • ⏳ Aftereffect: Remaining ticks the substance persists and is active in the plant's cells after synthesis stops.
  • ☠️ Lethality: The rate of population reduction per tick applied to swarms feeding on plants producing lethal toxins.
  • ⚡ Energy Cost: Active metabolic maintenance cost per tick incurred by plants while synthesizing the substance.

How to Apply Changes?

Modifying any value in a table field immediately triggers a background save to the server-side draft.

⚠️ Crucial: These edits will not affect the active running simulation loop until you click the 🔄 Load Draft button in the sticky top header, or restart/reset the simulation loop.
