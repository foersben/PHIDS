Diet Compatibility Guide

How the Matrix Works:

Determines which herbivore species can graze on which flora species.

  • Checked (Compatible): The herbivore swarm will actively consume plant biomass/energy when sharing a cell with this plant species, gaining energy.
  • Unchecked (Incompatible): The herbivore swarm ignores the plant and cannot feed on it, which may lead to starvation if no compatible food is available in the cell.

How to Apply Changes?

Toggling any checkbox immediately triggers a background save to the server-side draft.

⚠️ Crucial: These edits will not affect the active running simulation loop until you click the 🔄 Load Draft button in the sticky top header, or restart/reset the simulation loop.
