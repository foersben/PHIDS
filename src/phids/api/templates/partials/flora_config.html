Flora Species & Autotrophic Parameters

Ecological Parameters Explained:

  • ⚡ Base E (Base Energy): The initial biomass/energy allocated to new seedlings or placed specimens (min 0.1).
  • 🔋 Max E (Max Energy): The upper carrying capacity constraint per plant (min 0.1). Autotrophic growth ceases when saturated.
  • 📈 Growth Rate: Passive autotrophic energy gain per simulation tick (min 0.0).
  • ❤️ Surv. Thr. (Survival Threshold): The critical energy floor (min 0.0). Dropping below this triggers senescence (starvation death).
  • 🌱 Repr. Int. (Reproduction Interval): Tick interval dictating spatial dispersion attempts (min 1).
  • 🎭 Camo (Camouflage): Boolean toggle (on/off). Evolutionary trait causing localized chemical signal attenuation, disrupting the semiochemical flow-field and reducing olfactory detection.
