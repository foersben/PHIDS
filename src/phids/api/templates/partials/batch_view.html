📊 Diagnostic Diagram Guide

  • Balanced Overview / Stacked Biomass: Evaluates ecosystem energy transfer. Tracks Flora vs Herbivore population and biomass metrics to ensure energy is moving up the trophic chain and not becoming trapped.
  • Phase Space: Maps Flora Population (X) against Herbivore Population (Y). A stable Lotka-Volterra ecosystem forms a closed, repeating orbital loop. Crashing ecosystems spiral into the origin (0,0).
  • Collapse Risk Focus: Plots a survival probability curve (Kaplan-Meier estimator). Measures the exact probability that a scenario survives to tick T without hitting a terminal condition (Z2-Z7).
  • Herbivore Pressure Focus: Maps total herbivore population specifically against death_herbivore_feeding events (plant deaths caused by grazing) and death_starvation (herbivore deaths caused by lack of food). Proves whether declining flora populations are due to active grazing pressure or simply poor baseline growth constraints, and tracks when herbivores starve.
  • Defense Economy Ratio (Ledger): Isolates death_defense_maintenance events. Visualizes the metabolic burden of toxin synthesis to prove if flora are over-producing defenses and starving themselves to death.
